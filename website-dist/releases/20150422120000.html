Blahfe & Grpr Enters The Psychometrics Market with their Web Service


Service provides resources for people struggling with lack of ego formation, as well as networking and job opportunities.

Kodaira, TOKYO - Blahfe & Grpr announces today the official launch of the Utagaki ( https://utagaki.com/), a web service devoted to increasing awareness and understanding of stress issues, supporting ego formation that benefits those suffering from stress, and networking and job opportunities that help support qualifying life with significant mind management and other organizations that have been strong and effective leaders on issues consistent with the Company's mission. The Utagaki provides information and resources on stress and the suffering that it causes.

Blahfe & Grpr was founded by Nab Inno, MFA at Kanazawa College of Art. Nab plans to expand the Utagaki on a national level. "The Utagaki is committed to making a difference for this pain point." stated Nab. "With this new web service, we hope to provide a resource where individuals can learn more about various stress issues, and offer a way for people to give back to support this very worthwhile cause."
